Brand Story

The journey to net zero starts with a single route.

Route to Zero is a movement, a commitment, and a visual shorthand for the decarbonisation path the transport and energy sectors must travel. The identity captures momentum - a downward trajectory toward zero emissions, enclosed in the confidence of a road sign.

Brand Essence

The logo is rooted in two metaphors: the familiar shape of a road sign - communicating direction, purpose, and a clear destination - and a declining graph arrow traversing a globe, representing measurable progress toward a global goal.

Design Principles

Bold - High contrast, strong geometry, unapologetic weight.
Directional - Every element points toward zero.
Grounded - Industrial confidence, not aspirational softness.
Global - The sphere signals worldwide ambition.

Logo Misuse

Incorrect usage

Strict adherence to specifications maintains consistency.

Do not place on gradients that reduce contrast.
Do not distort or stretch. Scale proportionally.
Do not rotate. Always remain horizontal.
Do not reduce opacity or apply transparency.
Do not alter brand colours or apply filters.
Do not crop or cut off any element.
